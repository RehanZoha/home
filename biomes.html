BIOMES

Hover over different biomes to learn more!

Plains:
A flat biome with grass, flowers, and occasional trees.
Forest:
A biome with dense tree coverage, including oak trees.
Taiga:
A cold biome with coniferous trees like spruce and fir.
Desert:
A dry biome with little vegetation, featuring sand, cacti, and occasional desert temples or villages.
Savanna:
A grassy biome with scattered trees, including acacia trees.
Jungle:
A dense biome with tall trees, vines, and lush vegetation.
Snowy Tundra:
A cold biome covered in snow, with minimal vegetation.
Ocean:
A vast body of water with varying depths and underwater features.
Mountains:
A hilly biome with high peaks and rocky terrain.
Swamp:
A wetland biome with water, trees, vines, and lily pads.
